# Project Details

## Project Title:
Food Waste Reduction Platform

## Problem Statement:
Food waste is a major global issue where large amounts of edible food are discarded by households, restaurants, and supermarkets. This leads to economic loss, environmental damage, and missed opportunities to support people in need.

## Proposed Solution:
We propose a Food Waste Reduction Platform — a software system that connects food providers (restaurants, stores, and households) with consumers, charities, and organizations. The platform allows users to list surplus food in real time, making redistribution easier instead of wasting it.

## Project Scope:
- Aim: To reduce food waste by creating a digital platform that promotes efficient food sharing and redistribution.

- Objectives:
1. Enable users to easily list and find surplus food.
2. Connect donors (restaurants, stores, individuals) with receivers (charities, consumers).
3. Reduce environmental impact caused by food waste.
4. Encourage responsible consumption and community support.
5. Provide real-time updates and notifications for available food.

## Application Description:
The application is a platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting people who have surplus food with those who need it. Users can post available food items, browse listings, and receive notifications. The system ensures a simple, efficient, and accessible way to promote sustainability and community support.
