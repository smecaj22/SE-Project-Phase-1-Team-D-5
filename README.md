Problem Description
Food waste is a major global issue where large amounts of edible food are discarded by households, restaurants, and supermarkets. This leads to economic loss, environmental damage, and missed opportunities to support people in need.
Proposed Solution
We propose a Food Waste Reduction Platform—a software system that connects food providers (restaurants, stores, households) with consumers, charities, or organizations. The platform will allow users to list surplus food in real time, making it easier to redistribute instead of wasting it.
Aim
To reduce food waste by creating a digital platform that promotes efficient food sharing and redistribution.
Objectives
•	Enable users to easily list and find surplus food. 
•	Connect donors (restaurants, stores, individuals) with receivers (charities, consumers). 
•	Reduce environmental impact caused by food waste. 
•	Encourage responsible consumption and community support. 
•	Provide real-time updates and notifications for available food.
